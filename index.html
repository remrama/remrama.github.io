Hi, I'm Remy, a postdoctoral researcher in Ken Paller's lab at Northwestern University. My research focuses on memory, sleep, and dreams, with an emphasis on the characterization and potential benefits of lucid dreaming (when waking levels of cognition reappear during sleep). I recently finished a PhD in cognitive neuroscience from University of Texas at Austin working in the LewPea Lab.

Most of what I'm currently working on is touched on in this talk.
