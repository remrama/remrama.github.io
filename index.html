Hi, I'm Remy, a postdoctoral researcher in Ken Paller's lab at Northwestern University. My research focuses on sleep, dreams, and mood, with an emphasis on the characterization and potential benefits of lucid dreaming (when waking levels of cognition reappear during sleep). I recently received a cognitive neuroscience PhD from University of Texas at Austin while working in the LewPea Lab.
