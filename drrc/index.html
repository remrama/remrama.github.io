Dream Research Reading Club

[ future meetings | past meetings | subscribe to email list | faq ]

Dream research reading club is a reading or journal club where we meet for one hour per month and discuss a specific journal article about dreaming. We try to focus on recent publications.

Sign up for the email list (hit "Ask to join group"), where we send out the papers and Zoom link each month.

Reach out to me with any questions or paper suggestions.

Frequently asked questions

Who is welcome?
Anyone is welcome, at any time. It's mostly postdocs and graduate students right now, plus or minus a bit (undergraduate RAs and faculty). Once you join the google group you can view the list of group members.

How many people show up each month?
About 10.

What's the layout? Does a singular person present?
There is no singular presenter or a powerpoint presentation of any kind. We don't typically review the methods explicitly unless someone has specific questions. I will usually share the paper on screen and we bounce around, discussing figures and interpretations and such.

Do I have to say anything?
No. You can get involved in the discussion or listen in like a podcast. Typically if you turn your camera on at the start you'll have an opportunity to introduce yourself if you're interested, but I take camera off as a signal that you just want to listen in.
